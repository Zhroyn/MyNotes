## Keyboard
### Hotkeys
#### Modifier Symbols
- `#` Win
- `!` Alt
- `^` Ctrl
- `+` Shift
- `&` Used between any two keys or mouse buttons to combine them into a custom hotkey
- `<` Use the left key of the pair
- `>` Use the right key of the pair
